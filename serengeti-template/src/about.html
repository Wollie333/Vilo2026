About Serengeti Lodge

Discover the story behind Tanzania's premier luxury safari destination

Our Story

Founded in 2010, Serengeti Lodge was born from a passion for wildlife conservation and sustainable tourism. Our founders, lifelong nature enthusiasts, envisioned a place where luxury meets wilderness, where guests can experience the raw beauty of the Serengeti without compromising on comfort.

Over the years, we've grown from a small boutique lodge to one of Tanzania's most respected safari destinations, earning numerous awards for our commitment to excellence and environmental stewardship.

Today, we welcome guests from around the world, offering them an authentic safari experience that combines breathtaking wildlife encounters with world-class hospitality.
