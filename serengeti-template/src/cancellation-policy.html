Cancellation Policy

Last Updated: January 2026

1. General Cancellation Terms

At Serengeti Lodge, we understand that travel plans can change. This cancellation policy outlines the terms and conditions that apply when you need to cancel or modify your reservation. Please read these terms carefully before making your booking.

2. Cancellation Timeframes

The following cancellation fees apply based on when you cancel your reservation:

  • More than 60 days before arrival: Full refund minus a 10% administrative fee
  • 30-60 days before arrival: 50% refund of total booking amount
  • 15-29 days before arrival: 25% refund of total booking amount
  • Less than 15 days before arrival: No refund
  • No-show or early departure: No refund

3. How to Cancel Your Reservation

To cancel your reservation, you must:

  • Submit your cancellation request in writing via email to reservations@serengetilodge.com
  • Include your booking reference number and guest name
  • Cancellations are effective from the date we receive your written notification
  • You will receive a cancellation confirmation email within 24-48 hours
  • Refunds will be processed within 14 business days to the original payment method

4. Modification of Reservations

If you wish to modify your reservation dates rather than cancel, please contact us as soon as possible. Modifications are subject to availability and may incur additional charges. Changes made within 30 days of arrival will be treated as a cancellation and rebooking, subject to the cancellation terms outlined above.

5. Special Circumstances

We may waive cancellation fees in the following circumstances:

  • Serious illness or injury of the guest (medical certificate required)
  • Death of an immediate family member (death certificate required)
  • Natural disasters or extreme weather conditions that prevent travel
  • Government-issued travel restrictions or advisories

Documentation must be provided within 7 days of the cancellation. Each case will be reviewed individually.

6. Peak Season and Special Events

During peak seasons (June-October and December-January) and special events, stricter cancellation policies may apply. These will be clearly communicated at the time of booking. Peak season cancellations typically require 90 days' notice for any refund.

7. Group Bookings

Group bookings (5 or more rooms) are subject to separate cancellation terms which will be outlined in your group booking contract. Generally, group bookings require a non-refundable deposit of 30% and have extended cancellation notice periods.

8. Travel Insurance

We strongly recommend purchasing comprehensive travel insurance to protect your investment. Travel insurance can cover cancellation fees, medical emergencies, lost luggage, and other unforeseen circumstances. Serengeti Lodge is not responsible for costs associated with cancellations due to circumstances beyond our control.

9. Cancellations by Serengeti Lodge

In rare cases, we may need to cancel your reservation due to circumstances beyond our control (natural disasters, facility damage, safety concerns, etc.). In such cases, we will provide a full refund or offer alternative accommodation dates at no additional cost. We are not liable for any additional expenses incurred as a result of such cancellations.

10. Non-Refundable Deposits

All reservations require a deposit at the time of booking. For standard bookings, a 30% deposit is required. This deposit is non-refundable if cancellation occurs within 15 days of arrival. The remaining balance is due 30 days prior to arrival.

11. Early Departure

If you choose to depart early from your stay, no refund will be provided for unused nights. We recommend carefully considering your travel plans before booking.

12. Contact Information

For questions about our cancellation policy or to submit a cancellation request, please contact us:

  • Email: reservations@serengetilodge.com
  • Phone: +255 123 456 789
  • Hours: Monday - Friday, 8:00 AM - 6:00 PM EAT

13. Changes to This Policy

Serengeti Lodge reserves the right to modify this cancellation policy at any time. Changes will be posted on our website and will apply to bookings made after the date of the change. Existing reservations will be governed by the policy in effect at the time of booking.

By making a reservation with Serengeti Lodge, you acknowledge that you have read, understood, and agree to be bound by this Cancellation Policy.
