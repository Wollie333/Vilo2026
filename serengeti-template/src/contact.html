Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about visiting Serengeti Lodge

What is the best time to visit the Serengeti?

The Serengeti offers excellent wildlife viewing year-round. For the great migration river crossings, visit between June and October. For calving season, December to March is ideal. Each season offers unique experiences.

How far in advance should I book?

We recommend booking 3-6 months in advance, especially for peak season (June-October). This ensures availability and gives you the best selection of accommodations.

What's included in the room rate?

All our rates include accommodation, three meals per day, two game drives daily, park fees, and airport transfers. Beverages, spa treatments, and private guides are available at additional cost.

Is the lodge family-friendly?

Yes! We welcome families and have family suites available. Children under 12 stay at discounted rates. We also offer special children's programs and activities tailored for young explorers.
